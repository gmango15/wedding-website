Our Story

As a pair of casual gamers, Jollina and Glen first got to know each other through an online game. It wasn't until a group outing through mutual friends (thanks Gael and Allen) did they first see each other in person. They hung out and played Badminton that day. Glen was shy but already secretly interested. For Jollina, she would always say that for her it was love at first (Badminton) smash! They've been inseparable ever since!
